Over 64 of the worlds best male and female snowboarders and skiiers - many who will have competed at the winter olympic games in Korea in the “big Air” Slope Style competitiont - will compete over three days.

AIR + STYLE is a global Big Air nowboarding and skiing competition combined with a lifestyle music festival featuring world-class action sports athletes and musicians along with culture technology and fashion. In August 2018 Sydney will host an of cial Air + Style competition on a 16 story snow covered ramp built in The Domain.

Sydney has never seen anything like A+S before. Big ramp. Big sports stars. Big bands. And the biggest festival party right on the doorstep of the CBD and Sydney harbour.

The music lineup will be world class with previous A+S stages playing host to Major Lazer, Flume, Kendrick Lamar and Biffy Clyro.
